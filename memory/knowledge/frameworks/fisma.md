# FISMA — Federal Information Security Modernization Act

**Scope**: Federal information systems + contractors operating systems on behalf of federal agencies.

**Authority**: 44 U.S.C. § 3551 et seq. NIST is the SRM for security standards.

**Total controls**: NIST SP 800-53 Rev 5 baselines (Low ~125 / Moderate ~325 / High ~421). FedRAMP is the cloud subset.

## Control mappings

For each system: categorize per FIPS 199 (Low/Moderate/High Impact) → apply corresponding NIST SP 800-53 baseline. Edge platform mappings mirror those in [[fedramp]] for Moderate baseline.

## Signing officer

Authorizing Official (federal agency CIO/CISO). CSP side: CEO + designated POC.

## Audit cycle

Annual FISMA report to OMB. Continuous monitoring.

## Retention

Per federal records retention schedule + 3 years post-decommissioning.
