# ISO/IEC 27001:2022

**Scope**: Information Security Management System (ISMS). Voluntary certification; broadly recognized internationally.

**Authority**: ISO + IEC. Conformity assessment by accredited certification bodies.

**Total controls**: 114 in Annex A (2022 revision consolidated to 93 reorganized into 4 themes). Plus the management system clauses (4-10) for the ISMS itself.

## Annex A theme structure (2022 revision)

- A.5 Organizational controls (37)
- A.6 People controls (8)
- A.7 Physical controls (14)
- A.8 Technological controls (34)

## Management assertion / Statement of Applicability

ISO 27001 requires a Statement of Applicability (SoA) — for each Annex A
control, indicate Applicable/Not-Applicable and justification. ComplianceAgent
produces the SoA draft.

## Signing officer

CEO (RJain) for SoA + Management Review records. ISMS owner (CISO) for operational records.

## Audit cycle

Initial certification audit → annual surveillance audits → triennial recertification.

## Retention

Audit records: 3 years minimum. Management Review records: indefinite (improvement evidence).
